The problem

Have you ever wondered how fuel efficient your car is?

Do you really want to do the hard work of dividing your miles driven by the amount of gasoline you need when you are at a gas station? Of course not.

Calculating fuel economy is a problem that affects at least tens of people.

Imagine a world where this problem is erased for good. Imagine no longer. Presenting...

The solution

The Fuel Economy Predictor

The Fuel Economy Predictor, patent pending, is a remarkable new algorithm that will tell you how fuel efficient your car is with hardly any work.
